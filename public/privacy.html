Aegis Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

Aegis is an offline-first personal finance tracker built for India. Aegis operates with zero cloud servers, zero remote database sync, and zero tracking code.

1. Zero Data Collection

All bank SMS parsing, transaction extraction, loan schedules, spending charts, and voice summary announcements run entirely locally on your smartphone inside local JavaScript and SQLite storage.

2. SMS Permissions & Handling

  • Native Android APK Track: If installed via sideload APK, Aegis asks for SMS permissions to automatically read bank transaction SMS messages locally on-device. Your SMS messages are never uploaded to any remote server or third party.
  • Google Play Store Track: The Play Store web/PWA version requests zero SMS permissions. Users manually paste SMS text into the app.

3. Data Protection & Security

Sensitive documents, OTP verification codes, and financial summaries are secured with on-device AES-GCM encryption and Android FLAG_SECURE screenshot protection.

4. Data Ownership

You own your data completely. You can export all transaction logs to CSV or clear all stored database records at any time from the app Settings menu.

5. Contact Us

For questions or privacy inquiries, contact us at privacy@aegis.app.
